I'm a passionate Computer Science researcher specializing in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and healthcare technology innovation. My academic journey has been driven by a commitment to developing ethical AI solutions that address real-world challenges and create meaningful societal impact.

My research expertise spans AI-driven diagnostic systems, swarm robotics with distributed intelligence, and cybersecurity applications of machine learning, particularly in DNP3 intrusion detection. Through my thesis project "WeCare" - a cloud-native Electronic Health Record system - I demonstrated how AI-based diagnostic support can revolutionize clinical decision-making and healthcare accessibility.

With publications in AI-driven swarm robotics and autonomous cooperation systems, combined with hands-on experience in natural language processing and generative AI applications, I bring both theoretical depth and practical implementation skills to research initiatives. My work focuses on the intersection of artificial intelligence, healthcare innovation, and sustainable technology development.

I'm particularly interested in advancing research that bridges academic innovation with industry applications, contributing to the global scientific community through collaborative research projects and knowledge dissemination in emerging AI technologies.
